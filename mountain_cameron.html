Cameron Highland

   39000 Tanah Rata, Cameron Highlands, Pahang
  

About

The Cameron Highlands is famous for its flora and, as well as tea and strawberries, you will also find a huge number of species of roses here. If you like flowers then one of the best ways to see the roses is on a trip to Mrs.

  

What Can We Do?

Cameron Flora Park

There is a kiosk in the sea of flowers everywhere, and it is said that picnic packages will be launched for tourists to have a picnic on the spot, including Sweet and Simple, Dream Deluxe Picnic and Family Garden Picnic packages.

The Sheep Sanctuary

A sheep Farm in Cameron Highlands, located behind Kea Valley Hotel. A visit to The Sheep Sanctuary will leave yourself wishing to bring one home. A perfect place to unwind with your kids with great sceneary of Cameron Highlands and cool air. The Sheep Sanctuary tickets are very cheap, only RM8 for adults and RM5 for children, and a pack of sheep feed is included!

Cameron Centrum

Cameron Centrum is located in Brinchang, Cameron. There are European-style clocks and retro buses on the streets, making tourists feel like they are in Europe.

Restaurant Ferm Nyonya

If you want to eat hot pot, there is also a hot pot set menu for four! It is recommended that you try to avoid visiting this restaurant at 1:00 pm and 7:00 pm, because these times are the most crowded times in the restaurant!

Restoran Ferm Nyonya is well known to locals and tourist as a good place for good and affordable dishes. All our dishes are halal.
