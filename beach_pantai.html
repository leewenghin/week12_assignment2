Pantai Cenang

“Every day should feel like a day at the beach.”

Pantai Cenang, Langkawi

About

Cenang Beach was one of the Beautiful and clean beach at Langkawi. The beach is so beautiful, clean and there is so many water sport around.

What can we do?

Water Sports

If looking for an adrenaline rush during your stay in Pantai Cenang, Jet Ski Island Tour is a must. Visitors can go for swimming in the clean water lake, kayaking, trekking through the surrounding mountains and paddle boating.

Go-Karting

Experience go-karting in Pantai Cenang at the Morac Adventure Park. One can also learn go-karting and get to know about the safety rules.

Spa

Pantai Cenang offers a wide range of spas to choose from. Natural in-house ingredients are used to provide traditional massages. Each spa offers a variety of massages out of which the best can be chosen with the help of the therapists.

Nearby food

THE CLIFF RESTAURANT


Where white sandy beach meets the clear blue water of the Andaman Sea, The Cliff dazzles the senses with its ingenious contemporary design, charming views and beautiful ambiance – this is the island’s newest dining and drinking spot.


Opening Hours:

12pm - 10pm

Yellow Beach Cafe


Nice charming beach bar with indoor and outdoor seatings. Friendly owners. Couple from France and Ukraine. Service is friendly and food isn’t too bad. Tried the pizza and was lucky enough to try the owners Ukrainian beetroot salad.


Opening Hours:

5pm - 1am

El Toro Langkawi


Visit this restaurant to enjoy some great Mexican fare as you sit and watch the breathtaking sunset view. Good value for money, this is definitely our go to place for a proper Mexican meal when on the island. It is a great place for couples, families and groups.


Opening Hours:

12:30pm - 2am
