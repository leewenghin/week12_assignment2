Penang Hill

   Penang Hill, Pulau Penang
  

About

Penang Hill, also known as Penang Hill, is located in the central part of Penang Island. Its main peak, Western Hill, is 833 meters above sea level, which is the highest point in Penang. Among them, Qishan, which is 735 meters above sea level, is the most developed mountain among the peaks of Penang Mountain, and it is also a summer resort for local residents and tourists.

  

What Can We Do?

Hop off the flag mountain train

The most convenient way to get to Penang Hill is to take the train to the top!
Located at the foot of Penang Hill, this Swiss-designed train runs through the world's oldest train system, taking you through lush green forests.

Lock love

This love lock area is very popular (especially couples), but single people or with a group of friends also like to come here, you can take a bunch of IG photos!

Visit some historic buildings

If you are a history buff, you can embark on this historical trail journey to see some of the famous buildings on Penang Hill.

  

Food & Beverages

Kopi Hutan Cafe

Kopi Hutan Cafe is a great place to grab a cup of coffee and a delicious cake while surrounded by trees and fresh air for the perfect atmosphere!

It is worth mentioning that this coffee house, which is hidden in the forest and integrated with nature, is also a pitcher plant garden, with more than 100 kinds of exotic flowers, people can see different shapes, different colors, different species of Nepenthes.

Opening Hours:
9am–5pm





















Astaka/ Cliff Cafe

Cliff Cafe or Astaka Bukit Bendera is a three-storey food and beverage centre in Penang Hill. The view is particularly beautiful at night, when the city lights up.Cliff Cafe became operational January 2012.

The two higher levels houses the food court, with food stalls on the upper level and souvenir stalls and drinks stalls on the lower level.

Opening Hours:
8:00am - 8:00pm (Daily)





















The Habitat Shop & Cafe

Located within The Habitat Penang Hill, this café is an ideal spot to quench thirst and satisfy hunger after exploring the century-old tropical rainforest.

This is the perfect place to stay with your friends and family and it's time to enjoy a relaxing afternoon of planning.

Opening Hours:
9am–5pm
